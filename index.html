I'm Kyle, a dedicated software engineer working remotely for Asset Innovations.

I have spent the past few years working, designing, and building full stack web applications in Python, Java and JavaScript. I have had the pleasure of being able to open the eyes of new developers to the technologies used in the industry and the endless possibilities that can be done with code. From being able to bring a wireframe to life to building out the backend of an application, there's always something to be learned. That's just it, the thing I love most about being a software engineer is that I am always learning.

Where I came from

I am a veteran, I served in the Coast Guard as a maintenance technician on the Lockheed Martin C-130J model aircraft. I am a troubleshooter at heart and it shows. I made the switch to the tech industry after I separated from the Coast Guard and got married. I am a family man and want a career that is both challenging and rewarding, but also has a good work-life-balance.


Outside of work I am a husband and a father. I enjoy time with my family, the outdoors and working on my truck that is currently in the restoration process.
