I am first a husband and a father. Have been married since 2020 (yep, covid wedding) and a father since June 2023. I am a veteran, served in the Coast Guard. After I separated from the service I found a new love for technology. I attended Coding Dojo's software development bootcamp and learned to code in 3 fullstack languages. After gaining experience I was promoted to Senior Teaching assistant, then an Instructor. Outside of work I enjoy spending time with my wife and my little one. I am dedicated, hardworking and I cannot wait to see where the journey of this career takes me.
